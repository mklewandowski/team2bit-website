Tres Chic Wigs...
He did it again! On the eve of a big announcement, President Washington forgets his beloved stars and stripes wig! Join his faithful canine Dogsworth as he avoids deadly enemies and navigates his way to the top of the wall to return the missing wig. Packed with dozens of playable characters, zany enemies and obstacles, leaderboards, achievements, and more!
Washington's Wall is completely FREE and can be downloaded for iPhones and iPads in the App Store and for Android devices from Google Play.
