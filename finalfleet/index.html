Somewhere in the Horsehead Nebula...
A lone interstellar fleet must travel across a distant and perilous galaxy in the hope of reaching safety. Final Fleet is a roguelike VR space shooter, where players build and control a dwindling fleet of human spacecraft relentlessly pursued by an overwhelming alien force. Raid enemy outposts, hunt resources, upgrade your fleet, and above all: survive. Final Fleet is currently in early access and available for the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift through Steam VR.
